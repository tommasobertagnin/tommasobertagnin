Tommaso Bertagnin

2011 - UI DESIGN

SPORTIFY

Interface design for a mountain sports podcast

 
 

An engaging platform to follow your favourite sport and discover about new ones. Based on a unified search tool that leaves space to surprise, this application allows users to quickly generate playlist, and fill of sport stories their free time.

Thoughts - For a week we were tutored from two of the interface designers working at Spotify and thanks to them I discovered many new things. From the correct workflow necessary to generate consistent graphics, to the subtleties of UI design like soft shadows and reflections to easily guide the look of the user.

Tutors - Susann Stage, Stanley Wood
Tools used - Adobe Photoshop

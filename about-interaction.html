2012 - INTERACTION THEORY

ABOUT INTERACTION

A booklet on Interaction Design

 
 

With the recent introduction of software and digital devices, the creation of technology-driven solutions often originates products complex to use and that don’t reflect the actual needs of people. However, a necessary balance can be restored with the contribute of a proper discipline that deals seamlessly with technology and people: Interaction Design.

Tutors - Gillian Crampton Smith and Philip Tabor
Tools used - Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

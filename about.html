Tommaso Bertagnin



 

About

 

Between my Bachelors in Industrial Design and my Masters in Visual and Multimedia Communication, I worked for five years in product, game and web design. All these different influences made me focus on UX/UI Design. Now in my work I enjoy combining concepts, esthetics and technology to craft the future digital products.
